On April 28, 1916, Ferruccio Lamborghini,
the founder of the company that bears his name and is known for stylish, high-performance cars, is born in Italy.
Lamborghini's success enabled him to purchase a variety of luxury sports cars, including a Ferrari,
considered one of the top cars of the time.
